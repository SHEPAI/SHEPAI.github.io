Cookie policy

What are Cookies?

Cookies are data, stored in small text files, on your computer. When a web server has sent a web page to a browser, the connection is shut down, and the server forgets everything about the user. Cookies were invented to solve the problem "how to remember information about the user":

  • When a user visits a web page, his/her name can be stored in a cookie.
  • Next time the user visits the page, the cookie "remembers" his/her name.

What we store

The cookie will simply store text of conversations you've had with the AI, to make the AI smarter. The data consists of words.

Who can access the data

Only you can use this data, it is stored on your device. Other uses, and not even us can read your data. The protection of this data is down to the developer of your browser.

What we store

We store the words which you have spoken with the AI.

What is it used for

The data is simply to improve the performance of the system. The AI uses the words you teach it to speak with you. This data it only stores with your device, on our device.
