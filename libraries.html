Libraries

The SHEP project has been broken down into many different Python libraries. This means you can use the different abilities we have written for your own AI and robotics projects. Robotics and AI is complicated enough without you having to write the code for everything.

There are libraries for sound and information processing, in the future there will be image recognition, hardware control and more... Stay tuned! Follow our social medias or contact us to keep up to date!
